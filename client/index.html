Thiyagam Women Trust is registered under the Charitable Trust Act, (Regd No.900/06) established to serve the physically challenged women for their all round development with a touch of loving care and concern. The Trust is mainly focusing on the rural women with disabilities and empowers them to stand on their own legs. We create opportunities for self employment and provide environment to take up the challenges of life in an optimistic way with determination. We believe that the attitude of Positive ness coupled with a heart of abundance is the key to joyful life. We inculcate the attitude of gratitude in all our efforts and counsel the challenged women to count their blessings and happily share it with others. Read more

At Thiyagam Women Trust, we empower rural women with disabilities to stand on their own feet. Since 2005, we've been providing hope and support, thanks to the individuals and institutions who have supported us on this journey.


Ms. S J Amutha Santhy

Founder
